# 🛍️ Retail Sales Analysis for Business Intelligence | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes retail sales data from **2014 to 2017** to provide meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards. The project focuses on revenue, profitability, sales trends, product performance, country-wise analysis, sales representative performance, and business growth using advanced Power BI features.

The dashboard enables decision-makers to monitor KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and compare business performance across multiple dimensions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw retail sales data into an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard that helps stakeholders:

- Analyze Total Revenue, Total Cost, and Gross Profit
- Compare sales performance across countries
- Track Monthly (MoM) and Quarterly (QoQ) growth
- Identify top-performing products and sales representatives
- Analyze product categories and price ranges
- Support strategic business decisions using data

## 📌 Key Insights

- Germany generated the highest revenue and gross profit.
- Special category products contributed the largest share of sales.
- Medium-value products generated the highest overall revenue.
- High-value products produced the highest revenue per unit.
- Interactive filters allow dynamic comparison across countries and years.
- MoM and QoQ analysis helps identify seasonal business trends.
